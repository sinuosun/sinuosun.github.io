About me

Foundation

Mathematics as language

I am building a foundation in analysis, linear algebra, and physics, treating mathematics as a way to describe structure precisely.

Current curiosities include eigenvalues, geometric intuition, optimization, and the way abstract concepts become computational tools.

Engineering

Systems that touch reality

I am interested in embedded systems, sensors, robotics, and the small but concrete details that make hardware behave correctly.

I like projects where code meets signals, motion, constraints, and imperfect real-world feedback.

AI

Agents, cognition, and tools

I am exploring how AI agents can reason, interact, remember, and assist human creativity without replacing human judgment.

Earlier game-agent experiments are currently paused, but the broader question remains: how should intelligent tools cooperate with us?
